# Moonstar

This is a side-scrolling, action platformer game. Equipped with a sword and a shield, the player’s main goal is to make it to the end of the level. To do this, the player has to navigate through levels consisting of different paths and platforms, avoid traps, and fight enemies. If the player is killed, they have to try again from their last checkpoint.

Build link: https://charlesc32.itch.io/platformer-prototype

HUD behavior:
	Health indicators disappear when the player takes damage and reappear when they collect health pickups occasionally dropped by defeated enemies. 
	When the player reaches a new checkpoint, marked by a purple banner, text briefly appears informing the player that they have reached a checkpoint.

Controls:
- Hold LEFT CLICK and use MOUSE to look
- A and D to move left and right
- S to crouch
- SPACE to jump
- LEFT ARROW to draw sword
- RIGHT ARROW to attack
- UP ARROW to draw shield
- W to block upwards
- DOWN ARROW to "moonwalk"
- ESCAPE to pause
